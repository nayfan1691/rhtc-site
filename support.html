Financial support

We always welcome support for the theatre, be this attending performances or becoming a member of the theatre. We are registered as a charity so its easy to donate. Financial matters are handled by our Financial Director.

Please email for further information.

Gift in your will

Will you join the growing number of people who are supporting Robin Hood Theatre Company by leaving us a gift in your will? These gifts are vital to fund our increasing running costs and help us to provide community theatre.

Any money left to a charity is tax free and will therefore help to reduce the tax liability on your estate. This could save your beneficiaries money and at the same time help your community.

General support

Now the theatre is open, we have staged plans on the remainder of the work that needs to be completed, which also includes the ongoing maintenance. However anything that people can contribute will help ease the burden on our budget.

If you are a builder, plumber, electrician, plasterer etc, we need your skills. If you are not qualified in these trades, there is still plenty we can all do – painting, decorating, gardening, cleaning, tea and sandwich making etc. So fully qualified, time served tradesman, or keen enthusiast or just a willing volunteer….. we need your help.

Please contact the RHTC Secretary for more information.

200 Club

The 200 Club is a great way of supporting the theatre with the added bonus of winning a cash prize every month.

It costs £12.00 per number per year. Each month a number is drawn at random and if it’s your number you will win. 50% of the money per month is awarded to the winner.

There are still numbers available so please contact us and we will enter you in the draw. For more information download our application form or contact our 200 Club Administrator, Sue Smith.

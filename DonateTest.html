Robin Hood Theatre Company is looking forward to its next stage of development and continue to restore and rejuvenate our intimate theatre in the heart of the countryside.

Situated in the heart of Robin Hood’s county, Nottinghamshire, the theatre has a reputation for staging first class drama, offering premiers of new productions from award winning writers and also nurturing and developing new writing talent. Our aim is to develop the theatre to offer a quality experience for all our visitors and members. We are continuing to restore and develop our building to ensure our theatre’s continued success.

By choosing to make a monthly or one off donation you will be making a significant contribution to our theatre.

We recommend JustGiving because

  • Fundraising online is the best way for us to receive money.
  • Gift Aid is added automatically, which is an extra 25% on top of every eligible donation.
  • It reduced admin costs for us.
  • It's easier for you, there's no need to run around with a sponsorship form, collecting cash and cheques.
